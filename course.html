Theory of Change Part-1

Our students will be equipped with skills to pursue aspirational jobs like software engineering. They can choose to join the mainstream workforce or stay back in their communities and work remotely on opportunities which will be made available to them. They will become socio-economic centers in their families and communities.

Theory of Change Part-2

Our students will be using their skills to solve the problems that are plaguing their communities. From making simple apps to learn english to social network for farmers, there is a big role our alumni will play to bring technology to their communities, Our alumni will be an inspiration and examples for locals to understand how learning and opportunities are getting reinvented through Internet.
